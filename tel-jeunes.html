Tel-jeunes / branding

Tel-Jeunes is a non-profit organization dedicated to being a supportive presence around the clock. Tasked with reimagining the foundation's brand, my role encompassed everything from crafting the logo to designing the website. The essence of the identity flows seamlessly across all touchpoints. This project emphasized the importance of clarity, a minimalist approach, and vibrant colors that radiate positivity.
