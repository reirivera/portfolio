Festival spotify / UI design

The Spotify Festival is a fictional music event that offers a diverse lineup each year. The project allowed complete creative freedom for aesthetics and interactions. The goal of the project is to showcase the festival as dynamic and energetic. The images feature a vibrant color palette to represent the passion of the artists. Additionally, the website provides a variety of interactions for each image, offering useful information related to the event.
