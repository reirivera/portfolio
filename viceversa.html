vice versa / packaging

Sleek and minimalist, Vice Versa is a fictional perfume that tells the story of desire. The two products complement each other through their composition, graphic treatment, and colors, creating a perfect combination that can be appreciated by all. The typography was handcrafted using adhesive paper to accentuate the sinful nature of the concept.
