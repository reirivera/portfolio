War room / gameboard

War Room is a board game that encourages players to explore their human side while seeking to conquer and dominate their environment. Taking a satirical approach, the game's poster showcases vibrant and cheerful colors, yet unveils shocking images in each box, thereby highlighting the contrast between appearance and the consequences of war. The icons represent the recurring themes and consequences of a state of war, addressing aspects such as money, uprisings, conquest, and destruction. "War Room" invites players to step into the shoes of leaders and contemplate the implications of their actions.
