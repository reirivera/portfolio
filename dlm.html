Pupuseria DLM / branding

I had the incredible opportunity to explore my creative imagination and bring to life a unique restaurant concept. This is how I conceived a fictional pupuseria named DLM, where I could showcase and sell my very own artisanal hot sauce. DLM, the pupuseria of Montreal, holds a deep significance as it pays homage to my cherished childhood memories. Pupusas, traditional Salvadorian dishes, were a beloved part of my family's culinary tradition during my formative years. It was during this time, when my mom entrusted me with the freedom to design merchandise for the restaurant, that my profound passion for graphic design was ignited, marking the beginning of my remarkable journey in this field.
